About Us

Welcome to Sifa, your number one source for all things distribution. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of distribution services, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2007 by Solomon Gelle, Sifa has come a long way from its beginnings in 2007. When Gelle first started out, his passion for creating drove him to quit his day job and do tons of research so that Sifa can offer you the best distribution solutions. We now serve customers all over the region, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

About Sifa Distribution Limited

Sifa Distribution Limited is a leading distribution company dedicated to providing seamless and efficient supply chain solutions.
